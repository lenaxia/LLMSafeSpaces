## Contents of v0.2.1

**Bugfixes (1):**
- #505: deliver user-DEK secrets in pod-bootstrap; fix session-aware defer
  - RC1: user-DEK secrets are now returned by pod-bootstrap on cold boot
    when the workspace owner has an unwrappable jwt_sessions row. Pod boots
    with `GH_TOKEN` (and other user env-secrets) in opencode's env from PID 1
    instead of arriving 30 seconds to 2 hours later via the auto-push path.
  - RC2: session-aware restart no longer defers indefinitely on cold boot.
    Empty tracker is now treated as "not busy" (SSE is the sole busyness
    signal; `/session` records are historical), so credential updates apply
    immediately when opencode has no in-flight work.
  - Change 3: init-container materialize writes the reload cache, preventing
    a spurious auto-push per pod-recreate.
  - Change 5: `defaultMaxDefer` reduced 2h → 15m.
